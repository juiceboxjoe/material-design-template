Sentiguard

I contributed to this project as lead software engineer. SentiGuard is Community Policing meets modern smart phone. As part of a crime reporting suite the SentiGuard app lets users send reports or tips to subscribed law enforcement authorities, make live video calls to a command center with WebRTC, and more. Law enforcement officers access the data and interacrt with users through a webportal named SentiHelm.
